# Prompt: Sample Coverage Audit

You are auditing a small seed dataset for a genre-playbook experiment.

Task:

1. Identify what parts of the genre/category pattern are represented.
2. Identify what is missing or underrepresented.
3. Separate structural signals from author-specific expression.
4. Decide whether this sample is sufficient for Experiment 001.

Constraints:

- Do not reproduce source text.
- Do not infer private data.
- Do not analyze title formulas; titles are mostly series name plus number and are out of scope.

Output:

- Coverage summary
- Gaps
- Risks
- Recommendation: proceed / collect more

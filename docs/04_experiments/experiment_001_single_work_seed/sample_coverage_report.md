## Executive Recommendation

**Recommendation:** Proceed, but narrow scope.

The current 13 samples are strong enough to support a **Single-Work Seed Playbook v0** focused on mechanism hypotheses. They are **not** enough to support a fully generalized genre-wide playbook.

Suggested next document title:

> **Single-Work Seed Playbook v0: 金丝雀文学机制假设版**

Recommended disclaimer for the next-stage playbook:

> This playbook is derived from a single-work seed dataset and should be treated as hypothesis, not genre-wide rule. It is intended to extract transferable mechanisms for original creation, not to imitate the author or reproduce source-specific expression.

## Unsupported or Weak Dimensions

| Unsupported / Weak Dimension | Reason | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Cross-author genre rules | Only one author and one work are included. | Easy to mistake author-specific expression for genre-wide mechanism. |
| Cross-work structural generalization | No second work in the same category is included. | Cannot tell whether observed mechanisms are general to “金丝雀文学” or specific to this serial. |
| Complete long-form serialized architecture | Many posts are missing. | Can observe stage changes but cannot confidently summarize full beginning-middle-end structure. |
| Title formula | The title is fixed as series name plus number. | Should remain out of scope. |
| Cold-start opening mechanism | Posts 1 and 2 are missing. | Cannot judge how the work initially establishes protagonist, world, relationship, and follow-up desire. |
| Reader interaction / comment feedback | Dataset contains body text only. | Cannot infer which mechanisms drove platform distribution. |
| Update rhythm and viral nodes | Samples are not a complete sequence. | Cannot infer which turns corresponded to traffic growth. |
| Original generation quality validation | Current input is only source sample text. | Cannot yet prove reliable generation of original settings. |

## Numbering Irregularity

The irregular numbering does **not seriously affect this seed coverage audit**, because the current goal is not complete archiving or reconstructing the full serial. The goal is to test whether 13 seed samples are enough to extract preliminary mechanism hypotheses.

However, irregular numbering weakens:

| Affected Item | Impact Level | Explanation |
|---|---:|---|
| Long-form serialized structure | High | Too many gaps prevent confident reconstruction of the full serial rhythm. |
| Character growth arc | Medium-high | Transformation is visible, but not all key turning points are identifiable. |
| Foreshadowing and payoff | High | With skipped posts, it is hard to know which details are deliberate foreshadowing. |

## Supplement Recommendation

Supplementing samples is recommended, but not mandatory.

If the goal is only to create a **narrow-scope single-work playbook draft**, it is acceptable to proceed without additional samples.

If the goal is to validate a **transferable RedNote fiction category playbook**, additional sampling is recommended.

| Supplement Type | Suggested Count | Why |
|---|---:|---|
| Cold-start samples | 2 posts: ideally posts 1 and 2 | Current samples do not show how the work first establishes protagonist, world, relationship, and follow-up motivation. |
| Continuous middle samples | 3–5 posts, ideally from posts 16–31 or 33–55 | Current middle structure has large gaps. Continuous samples would show how conflict escalates step by step. |
| High-engagement samples | 3 posts | If engagement is visible, these can help identify stronger platform mechanisms. |
| Same-category different-author samples | 5–8 posts | Key to separating category mechanisms from single-author features. |

## Minimal Supplement Plan

Recommended minimum if collecting more: **8–10 additional posts**.

Priority order:

1. Add posts **1 and 2** to solve the cold-start gap.
2. Add **3–5 continuous middle posts** to strengthen long-form structure analysis.
3. Add **3 same-category posts from another author/work** to reduce single-author overfitting.

Lightweight option:

- Posts 1 and 2
- Any 3 continuous middle posts

## Final Recommendation

**Proceed, but narrow scope.**

The current dataset is sufficient for:

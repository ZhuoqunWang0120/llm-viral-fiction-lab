# Ethics and Boundaries

## Collection boundaries

- Only collect visible text available in the logged-in browser UI.
- Do not use private APIs.
- Do not reverse engineer signatures.
- Do not bypass CAPTCHA, login restrictions, anti-bot protections, or rate limits.
- Do not collect images, videos, comments, profile data, follower data, or hidden/private data.
- Use slow, small-batch collection only.
- Stop immediately if risk-control, CAPTCHA, login challenge, abnormal traffic, or access restriction appears.

## Data boundaries

- Do not publish raw collected text.
- Do not commit session files, cookies, tokens, browser profiles, screenshots, page HTML, or debug artifacts.
- Keep raw and processed sample data local.

## Creative boundaries

- Do not imitate a specific author.
- Do not reproduce source text, specific lines, specific scenes, character names, or distinctive personal expression.
- Study genre/category mechanisms only.
- Generate original characters, original settings, and original plots.

# Ethics and Boundaries

- Study genre/category mechanisms, not author imitation.
- Do not reproduce source text.
- Do not publish raw collected samples.
- Do not bypass platform controls, login challenges, CAPTCHA, rate limits, or risk-control pages.
- Collect only visible text available in the logged-in browser UI.
- Do not collect comments, images, videos, profile data, follower data, or hidden/private data.
- Keep session files and local outputs out of Git.
